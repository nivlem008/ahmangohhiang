Our Heritage Story

For over 40 years, our family has been preserving the authentic taste of traditional ngoh hiang, passed down by our beloved grandmother (Ah Ma).

Each piece of ngoh hiang is carefully marinated using premium sauces, creating a perfect balance of flavors - meaty and loaded with fresh prawns, combined with savory crunchiness and a delicate sweetness from carefully selected vegetables.

We believe in honoring our heritage while sharing these timeless flavors with food lovers across Singapore. Every bite tells the story of our family's dedication to quality and tradition.
